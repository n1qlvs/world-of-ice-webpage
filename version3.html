All About Penguins!

Macaroni Penguin

The Macaroni penguin is found in the Subantarctic and Antarctic Peninsula. They have yellow-orange crests and feed on krill.

African Penguin

Native to southern Africa, these penguins make loud, donkey-like calls and are currently endangered.

All About Arctic Foxes!

White Morph Arctic Fox

The White Morph Arctic Fox changes its fur color seasonally and thrives in extreme cold conditions.

Blue Morph Arctic Fox

The Blue Morph Arctic Fox has a dark bluish-gray coat and lives in coastal areas.
